Equillibrium in life

Often, work takes precedence over everything else in our lives. Our desire to succeed professionally can push us to set aside our own well-being. Creating a harmonious work-life balance or work-life integration is critical, though, to improve not only our physical, emotional and mental well-being, but it's also important for our career.
In short, work-life balance is the state of equilibrium where a person equally prioritizes the demands of one's career and the demands of one's personal life. A good work-life balance, has numerous positive effects, including less stress, a lower risk of burnout and a greater sense of well-being.
Through our unique program we emphasize the importance of balancing mental, physical, emotional and social health of every individual.

Our Approach

Empowering, not educating

We empower everyone as a team. From school management to teachers to school staff to students and parents, our curriculum empowers everyone towards healthier and experiential learning environment.


360o Feedback Model

Our curriculum and courseware involve 360o Feedback Model where students can give feedback about their learning after every class.

Track Progress and celebrate success

Our courseware includes progress trackers for everyone to self- evaluate their learnings. Recognizing your accomplishments can help maintaining momentum, identify opportunities to grow, plan for sustainable long-term change and celebrate success by awarding themselves with self-created certificates.

School-Parent Partnership Model

A recent survey found that while 86% of parents are interested in providing input on kids’ health and learning issues, 51% of parents do not feel included in decisions about children’s health and well-being at their schools. We aim to bridge that gap through our curriculum.
Our 3-step framework for healthier schools helps leveraging School - Parent partnerships to build safe and supportive learning environments where children can play, learn, and grow.
