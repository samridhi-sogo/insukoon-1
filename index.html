Online and Hybrid Training

Pandemic has radically shifted the culture of our schooling model. Online education is rolling out everywhere, which might put a cloud on teaching strategies for active participation of the learners. But now is the time for intelligent collective action.

Here are ‘High Five’ reasons that make our online model no less than classroom teaching:

Active Learning Lesson Plan

Impactful lesson for instant engagement and participation

Interactive Teaching

We don’t follow the trend of video-based teaching, instead we use prop-based teaching methods to promote creativity and imagination.

@Home Effect

Being with family, it’s easy to create new habits and a mindful culture change in the home environment.

360° Feedback Model

Our curriculum and courseware involve 360° Feedback Model where students can give feedback about their learning after every class.

Progressive Tracking Mechanism

Our courseware includes progress trackers to self-evaluate their learning and celebrate success by awarding themselves with certificate offered under program.
