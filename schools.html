Why Schools?

Kids spend almost 1200 hours in school. The school community – including teachers, students, parents, staff mutually provide an environment to support happier, healthier kids.
At INSUKOON, we believe in creating healthier schools by bringing all the members of a school community together and equipping them with the tools and resources they need to make change happen. We leverage parents-school partnership to build healthier communities where kids thrive.
If you’re looking for a compassionate, effective and collaborative approach for the better learning of the students, you’ve come to the right place.

Let’s get started!!!

Introducing Savoir Science

Kids do well when offered a perfect atmosphere rather than perfect resources.

INSUKOON - introducing for the first time ever – a groundbreaking non-academic subject – ‘SAVOIR SCIENCE’ to be taught in every school across the globe year after year.
We emphasize the importance of teaching non-academic subject along with the regular subjects and making Savoir Science a part of the student's daily time table.
Our curriculum addresses ‘The Need of The Hour’ topics highlighting competency nurturing and promoting alternate talent for students, which we firmly believe is ‘Future of Education’.
We are focused to inculcate skills like “Imagination”, “Innovation”, “Conceptualization”,” Visualization” and infusing First Principle Thinking.
But that’s really just the tip of the iceberg.
Our courseware offers prime quality hardcover books (set of 3 for every kid) designed in an incremental and meaningful manner to unleash one’s true potential.

Teachers' Empowerment Program(TEP)

Interacting with kids take patience, practice and persistence.

TEP is a systematic approach designed to fortify and enhance mindfulness and development of skill, attitude, knowledge and behavior patterns in teachers. This easy-to-follow program of 10 sessions (once every month for two hours) offers teachers an opportunity to create, recognize, experience and reinforce a calming presence within themselves leading to better productivity and work life.
TEP focuses on building Cross Curricular Professional Development (CCPD), Mindful strategies for better classroom management, different teaching models, tools and techniques, health management (mental, physical, social, emotional), promoting progressive extremism and much more…
For measuring evidence of impact, data collection tools, concept quizzes and course evaluations are all vital to this comprehensive training.


To know more about our program and pricing, please contact us

Staff Enrichment Program(SEP)

We take care of those who take care of our kids at school, making schools a secure place of learning.

Not only teachers, the culture and behavior of the whole staff helps building a positive environment in school. We believe in ‘No one is left behind’ approach. We are happy to offer a complimentary program (SEP) for all the staff of the school.

Parents' Engagement Program(PEP)

Partnering instead of parenting

Parents' behavior presents powerful lessons to a child and leaves impressions on the developing mind.
At INSUKOON, We firmly believe parents contribute 70% of child’s growth and development and so we are happy to offer a complimentary training program for parents enabling them to guide and support actively in the development of their child, understanding child’s interests, dreams, emotions and behavior in a more synchronized way.
Our program structure includes two training sessions and two feedback sessions for the parents addressing parenting challenges and solutions, strengthening parent-child relationship, child’s brain nutrition, promoting alternate talent in child, overcoming child’s learning difficulties, resources to be provided to child for their overall development and much more…
‘Parents, are you ready to give a lifetime valuable gift to your child?'
